FGEHA - Resident Service Portal

This page explains how users can request account deletion and deletion of associated data for the FGEHA - Resident Service Portal app.

How to request account deletion

  • Fill out the request form below and click Send request.
  • If your email app does not open, send the same details manually to the support email.
  • Include the account email used in the app so we can locate your account.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile and authentication records linked to your app account.
  • Submitted service request history linked to your account, unless legal retention is required.
  • Uploaded request attachments (such as photos) linked to your account, unless retention is required.

What data may be retained

  • Security, audit, and fraud-prevention logs required for legal or operational purposes.
  • Records that must be retained to comply with applicable laws and public service obligations.

Standard deletion processing time: up to 30 days from successful identity verification.

Submit deletion request

Support email: support@fgeha.gov.pk

Contact

For questions about account deletion or privacy, contact support@fgeha.gov.pk.
